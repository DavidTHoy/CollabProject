Scorecard


Technical/Business Proficiency: Does the team exhibit the expected level of technical and business knowledge?*

Does not meet expectations
  • Does not demonstrate expected knowledge
  • Cannot articulate clear answers to questions
  • Is not able to apply knowledge to a problem scenario
Meets Expectations
  • Demonstrates the expected level of knowledge
  • Is able to clearly articulate answers and explanations to questions
  • Is able to apply knowledge appropriately to a problem scenario


Communication: Is the team able to clearly and concisely explain technical concepts?*

Does not meet expectations
  • Cannot articulate clear answers to questions
  • Explanations are convoluted and difficult to follow
Meets Expectations
  • Is able to clearly articulate answers and explanations to questions
  • Explanations are direct and easy to understand


Time Management: Is the team able to work within the customer’s time constraints?*

Does not meet expectations
  • Cannot answer questions in the time allotted
  • Pace is poor – speaks too quickly or too slowly
  • Deviates from topic and loses focus
Meets Expectations
  • Accurately explains concepts in the time allotted
  • Pace is appropriate
  • Stays focused and on topic


Professional Demeanor: Is the team able to build a relationship and present a professional demeanor to the customer?*

Does not meet expectations
  • Extreme nervousness and lack of confidence
  • Appearance is inappropriate for a customer presentation
  • Dishonest, unwilling to say, "I don't know", making up answers
Meets Expectations
  • Calm and confident
  • Appearance is appropriate for customer presentation
  • Shows integrity and honesty, demeanor breeds trust and respect, is able to admit when they do not know the answer
